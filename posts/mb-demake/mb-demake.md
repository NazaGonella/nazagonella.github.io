## Mount & Blade: Demake

November 17, 2025

---

### Battles

- Bump melee.
- Asymetric units based on terrain.
- Archer units deal 1.5 times the base damage when attacking from mountain tile.
- Cavalry units deal 0.75 times the base damage when attacking from mountain tile.
- Cavalry units deal 1.75 times the base damage when attacking from a plain tile to a plain tile.
- All units deal 0.75 times the base damage when attacking from a plain tile to a mountain tile.
